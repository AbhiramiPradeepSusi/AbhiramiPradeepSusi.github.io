Zapier Automation Workflow for Lead Management

Created an automated workflow in Zapier to streamline the lead management process. The workflow includes the following steps:

  1. Trigger: New lead added to the CRM.
  2. Action: Send a welcome email to the new lead.
  3. Action: Add the lead to a specific email marketing list.
  4. Action: Create a task in the CRM for follow-up.
  5. Action: Notify the sales team via Slack about the new lead.
  6. Action: Integrate chatGPT AI to send customised message template to the sales team
This automation ensures that new leads are promptly welcomed, added to the marketing list, and assigned for follow-up, enhancing lead engagement and conversion rates.

#1 Tools Used

  1. Zapier
  2. CRM (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce)
  3. Email Marketing Platform (e.g., Mailchimp)
  4. Slack
  5. chatGPT AI
